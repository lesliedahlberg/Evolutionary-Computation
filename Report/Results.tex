\subsection{Machine Learning}

The algorithms were tested on four machine learning benchmarks: $ML_{1}$, $ML_{2}$, $ML_{3}$ and $ML_{4}$ as described in the section ``Method''. The dimensionality of the problem depends on the size of the input and output vectors of the data-sets and the size of the hidden layer of the neural networks. The hidden layer size was defines as the mean of the input- and output-layer sizes.
